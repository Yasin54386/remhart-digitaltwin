About REMHART

REMHART is a state-of-the-art renewable energy research facility at Charles Darwin University's Energy and Resources Institute (ERI). Our smart grid management system combines cutting-edge digital twin technology with real-time monitoring and AI-powered analytics.

Key Features:

  • Real-time grid monitoring and visualization
  • AI-powered predictive maintenance
  • Energy flow optimization
  • Data-driven decision support
  • Advanced analytics and forecasting
