# Logo Images

Please place your logo image files in this directory:

1. **cdu-logo.png** - CDU (Charles Darwin University) logo
2. **eri-logo.png** - ERI (Energy and Resources Institute) logo

## Recommended specifications:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Height: Approximately 120-150px (will be auto-scaled to 60px in the header)
- Width: Auto (maintain aspect ratio)

The logos will appear in the header of all pages:
- CDU logo on the left
- ERI logo with "ERI" and "Energy and Resources Institute" text in the middle
